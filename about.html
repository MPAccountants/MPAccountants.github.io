About MP Accountants

As a leading accounting firm for more than 33 years, Martinez & Perdomo Professional Association has excelled in providing a wide range of financial solutions to businesses and individuals in South Florida. We understand our clients’ need for high-quality, personalized services to better manage their accounting, bookkeeping, investments and taxes.

Every client has different goals and needs for themselves or their organization — our aim is to provide tailored accounting solutions to every client in a responsible and effective manner. From tax preparation and analysis of financial statements to new business formation and representation before the IRS, we work closely with you while guiding you every step of the way in order to help your business or personal finances become more productive, streamlined, and efficient.
