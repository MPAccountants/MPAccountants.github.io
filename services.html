Martinez & Perdomo PA is offering Business Accounting, Business & Personal Taxes and Consulting services as explained in the sections below.

Business Accounting

As a business owner, handling your own bookkeeping and accounting can be time-consuming and difficult. That’s why Martinez & Perdomo P.A. is dedicated to support your business with custom-fit accounting services that save you time and effort, and give you a precise financial picture of your organization — providing you with the peace of mind to concentrate on growing your business.

Our services include:

  • Bookkeeping
  • Preparation of financial statements
  • Internal auditing
  • Payroll services
  • Business reports
  • Filing of corporate state and federal taxes

Business & Personal Taxes

Filing income taxes is more than just reporting your earnings — it entails knowing your tax obligation and the proper advantages the law offers so that you can better plan for the future. The ever-changing tax laws can be overwhelming, so we take on that responsibility for you, with the aim to lower your tax liability and educate you on tax reduction strategies. We offer you:

  • Preparation and filing of personal income taxes
  • Profit and loss statements
  • Tax obligation consultation

Consulting

In addition to your concerns regarding tax and accounting, you may have questions regarding more complex financial transactions and situations. Our team is ready to advise you on a number of issues so that you can maximize your resources, forecast, and organize your overall business outlook. Our consulting services include:

  • Debt management
  • Asset management
  • Self-employment
  • Money management
  • New business formation
